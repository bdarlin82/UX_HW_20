RX Vacations

Mobile App Case Study

My Role: Lead UX Designer

Duration: March/April 2023

Tools Used: Figma/Miro/Zoom

Project Overview

Conduct research and discover data that pertained to traveling as/with an immuno compromised traveler. We condcted a series of interviews via zoom with a target audience of people who travel a significant amount during or after the pandemic. After gathering the data and organizing the information it was time to create an app that would allow the user an organized space to hold their medical information, and guide them to places that were up to date with post-pandemic policies. Essentilally, Rx Vacations would be an all in one travel app where you could not only search for destinations, but book tours and accomadations as well, all while beeing kept up to date with all travel requirements and covid policies. It would be an essential tool for an immuno compromised traveler.

Defining The Problem

We conducted our user research via zoom interviews. My interview partner and I asked questions regarding how one feels traveling in this post pandemic era. We gained insight into whether or not they feel safe or unsafe while traveling.

Interview Quote 1

“We had a concierge service when I worked for my London office, but it really wasn’t that great. It was three or four times more because of concierge fees.” Emily Van Age:40 Global Operations

Interview Quote 2

“When I went to Mexico two years ago my sister had a cancer treatment. I accompanied her and we had to be masked up the whole time. It was hot and it was humid, and it was no fun” Gayle Sisson Age: 73 Semi-Retired

Interview Quote 3

“We usually rent Airbnb because we think that’s a better deal for your money. We all enjoy cooking, so that’s never an issue. Or we’ll go out and eat in the local restaurants and stuff.” Karen Zacjew Age: 65 Chef

Process

With interviews and collected data we can begin the process of mapping out the users goals and the focus of our mobile app.

Research And Discovery

Our interview data is mapped out to discover our ultimate user. They become a tangible idea, and the persona emerges that will introduce us to new solutions of travel in a post pandemic world.

Meet The User

The scenario is yet, and our user persona is traveling to New Zealand with his immuno compromised sister. Rx Vacations is their guide in place to help them along the way. Our initial sketches show a clean calm aesthetic that will aide in the efficiency of use. We don't want our user to become confused with the excess. Early wireframing organizes the process and helps eliminate any unwanted design structures.

Prototypes
The first prototype was the most basic idea of images and bodies of text. User testing revealed an intuitive sense of where to navigate to, and this helped gain insight into where certain buttons and icons should be.
Looking Towards The Future

The future of Rx Vacations relies on the full development of the medical wallet, and the ability have up to date knowledg of mask mandates and covid protocols. Peace of mind for the immuno compromised user and their companions is the continued goal of this project.
